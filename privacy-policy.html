Introduction

EDScan ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

1. Account Information

  • Email address (collected during Google Sign-In for authentication)
  • Account creation date (automatically recorded)

2. Usage Data

  • Scan usage counts (number of ingredient scans performed)
  • Subscription status (free or premium)

3. User-Generated Content

  • Saved products: If you choose to bookmark a product, we store the ingredient text and analysis results (danger, warning, and safe ingredient counts)

4. Camera Access

  • Camera permission is used to capture images of product ingredient labels
  • Important: Scanned images are processed in real time and are not stored on our servers. Only extracted text is used for analysis.

How We Use Your Information

  • Authentication and account access
  • Ingredient analysis and database matching
  • Managing scan limits for free and premium users
  • Storing bookmarked products for user reference
