
# Privacy Policy for EDScan
**Effective date:** January 3, 2026

Developer: NextGen Codemasters

Contact: codemasters.ad@gmail.com

Introduction
EDScan ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect
1. Account Information

Email address: Collected during Google Sign-In for authentication purposes
Account creation date: Automatically recorded when you create an account
2. Usage Data

Scan usage counts: Number of ingredient scans performed
Subscription status: Whether you have a free or premium subscription
3. User-Generated Content

Saved products: If you choose to bookmark a product, we store the ingredient text and analysis results (danger, warning, and safe ingredient counts)
4. Camera Access

We request camera permission to capture images of product ingredient labels
Important: Scanned images are processed in real-time and are NOT stored on our servers. Only the extracted text is used for analysis.
How We Use Your Information
Authentication: To verify your identity and provide access to your account
Service delivery: To analyze ingredients and cross-reference them against EU endocrine disruptor databases
Usage management: To track scan limits for free and premium accounts
Product saving: To store your bookmarked products for future reference
Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services:

Google Sign-In: For authentication (subject to Google's Privacy Policy)
Google Cloud AI (Gemini): For extracting ingredient text from images (images are processed but not stored)
Supabase: For secure data storage and authentication
Google Play Billing: For processing premium subscriptions
