Introduction

EDScan ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

EDScan helps you identify potential endocrine-disrupting chemicals in product ingredients by scanning ingredient lists with your camera.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address: Collected through Google Sign-In for account identification
  • Account creation date: For account management purposes

Usage Data

  • Scan count: Number of ingredient scans performed (for usage limits)
  • Subscription status: To manage premium features

User-Generated Content

  • Saved products: Product names and ingredient lists you choose to save

Camera Access

  • We access your camera to scan ingredient labels
  • Important: Images are processed in real-time and are NOT stored on our servers

How We Use Your Information

  • Authentication: To verify your identity and manage your account
  • Service delivery: To analyze ingredients and provide disruption risk information
  • Usage management: To track scan usage for free/premium tier limits
  • Product saving: To allow you to save and retrieve product information

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In: For authentication (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Google Cloud AI (Gemini): For ingredient text recognition
  • Supabase: For secure data storage (Supabase Privacy Policy)
  • Google Play Billing: For subscription management

Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored securely using Supabase with encryption
  • Images captured for scanning are processed locally and NOT transmitted or stored
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information
  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption

Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active
  • Saved products: Retained until you delete them or request account deletion
  • Usage data: Reset periodically based on subscription cycle

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-out: Stop using the service at any time

To request deletion of your account and data, please visit our Account Deletion page.

Children's Privacy

EDScan is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
